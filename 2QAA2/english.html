FEATURED WORKS:
Nexus Academy is proud to announce the outstanding winners of our recent Sonnet-Making Competition, a showcase of literary talent and creativity that illuminated the brilliance of our student community. The competition, which invited students to craft sonnets that blend eloquence, emotion, and artistry, drew forth a remarkable array of entries, making the judging process both challenging and inspiring.
