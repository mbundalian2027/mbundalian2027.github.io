EVENTS HUB
At Nexus Academy, we believe that education extends beyond the classroom, and our vibrant community comes together through a tapestry of events that inspire, engage, and foster a sense of belonging. The Events Hub is your gateway to a myriad of activities that enrich student life, encourage creativity, and celebrate the diverse talents within our school community.


Celebrating Nexus Academy's Foundation Day: A Journey of Excellence
Nexus Academy is buzzing with excitement as we commemorate our Foundation Day - a day that marks the genesis of our institution's commitment to education, innovation, and community. On this special occasion, we reflect on the remarkable journey that has unfolded since our inception.

The day kicks off with a sense of nostalgia as alumni, faculty, and current students come together to reminisce about the early days. It's a time to honor the dedication of our educators and the enthusiasm of our students who have made Nexus Academy a thriving hub of knowledge.

Gathering with fellow students, faculty, and families, we came together for a heartwarming Christmas Tree Lighting Ceremony. The beautifully decorated tree illuminated the night, symbolizing the spirit of unity, joy, and hope that characterized both the holiday season and Nexus Academy's foundation.



Exploring the Rich Tapestry of the Philippines at Nexus Academy's Open House
Nexus Academy recently opened its doors to an unforgettable journey of cultural exploration during our Open House event. Themed "Philippine Odyssey," this celebration of diversity immersed our school community in the vibrant and multifaceted tapestry of Filipino culture.

The event showcased various facets of the Philippines, transporting attendees to different regions through immersive exhibits, performances, and interactive activities. From the bustling streets of Manila to the serene landscapes of Palawan, every corner of the school was transformed into a unique showcase of Philippine heritage.



Nexus Academy's Family Day Extravaganza: A Day of Togetherness and Fun
Nexus Academy recently hosted its much-anticipated Family Day, a joyous occasion that brought together students, faculty, and families for a day filled with laughter, games, and heartwarming moments.

Sports enthusiasts found their haven on the playing fields where friendly competitions unfolded. Volleyball matches, tug-of-war, and relay races showcased not only athletic prowess but also the spirit of camaraderie that defines Nexus Academy's close-knit community.
