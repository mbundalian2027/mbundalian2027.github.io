Academy Launches Innovative Coding Program for Students:
Date: Dec 16, 2023


Nexus Academy is excited to announce the launch of a cutting-edge coding program aimed at empowering students with essential tech skills. The program, developed in collaboration with industry experts, offers hands-on coding experiences, project-based learning, and opportunities to work on real-world applications. Students will delve into programming languages, web development, and software engineering, preparing them for the rapidly evolving digital landscape.
Nexus Academy Celebrates Science Fair Success:
Date: Dec 20, 2023


Nexus Academy students showcased their scientific prowess at the annual Science Fair, leaving a trail of innovation and creativity. The event featured a diverse range of projects, from eco-friendly solutions to breakthroughs in healthcare technology. Judges praised the students for their dedication and originality, and several projects have been selected to represent the school at regional science competitions. Congratulations to all the young scientists at Nexus Academy!.
Nexus Academy's Model United Nations Team Wins Top Honors:
Date: Dec 30, 2023


Nexus Academy's Model United Nations (MUN) team achieved remarkable success at the recent international conference. Representing various countries, our students engaged in diplomacy, negotiations, and debate on global issues. The team received multiple awards for their outstanding performance, demonstrating their prowess in public speaking, research, and international affairs. Nexus Academy continues to foster global citizenship through enriching experiences like MUN.
