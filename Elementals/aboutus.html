Our game concept was inspired by Pokemon. We took the turn-based combat and luck-based mechanics of Pokemon and incorporated them into our own game. Players engage in strategic battles where they take turns commanding their characters to use various skills and abilities.

Similar to Pokemon, each character in our game has unique abilities and attributes, and players must carefully choose their next move to outmaneuver their opponents. However, we've added our own twist to the game by introducing different characters, skills, moves, and passives.
