ELEMENTALS


RULES:


ELEMENTALS IS A STRATEGICAL PLAYER VS PLAYER GAME


1. EACH PLAYER CHOOSES THEIR ELEMENTAL ATTRIBUTE WHICH HAVE THEIR OWN PASSIVE
• Fire: High offensive capabilities with no heal skill
Hell's Inferno: 12 DMG
Scorching Sun: 17 DMG | 25 MANA
Hellfire's Incarnate: 40 DMG | 80 MANA

Passive: Burning Soul - Deal extra damage equals to 30% of lost HP


• Water: An all rounder with decent damage and heal
Striking Tide: 10 DMG
Hydro-Shield: +30 HP | 20 MANA
Drowning Wave: 25 DMG (+10% of enemy's HP) | 45 MANA

Passive: Sea Cradle - Gains an extra +10 heal if HP drops below 30
Active Passive: Neptune's Gift - Permanent 20 mana gain


• Earth: Tanky with massive heals but low on offense
Groundbreaker: 7 DMG
Earthen Wall: +50 HP | 40 MANA
Geo Grandia: 20 DMG | 45 MANA

Passive: Full Recovery - If HP drops below 50, "Earthen Wall" won't require mana


• Wind: Balanced offensive and defensive capabilities

Towering Tornado: 15 DMG
Goddess's Breath: +20 HP | 30 MANA
Tempest Drive: 30 DMG | 50 MANA

Passive: Windtalker - Heals +30% of damage dealt to the enemy in every attack
Active Passive: Faith or Luck? - All skills have a high chance of working



2. EACH ELEMENT HAVE 3 DIFFERENT SKILLS (1 BASIC AND 2 SPECIAL) WITH 2 DIFFERENT EFFECTS (HEAL AND DAMAGE)


3. EACH ATTACK WILL GO THROUGH A RANDOMIZER TO DETERMINE WHETHER IT WILL BE EFFECTIVE OR NOT (EACH MOVE HAS DIFFERENT CHANCES OF WORKING)


4. PLAYERS HAVE LIMITED MANA TO PREVENT SPAMMING (BASIC ATTACKS REPLENISH 10 MANA PER MOVE)


5. EACH PLAYER WILL TAKE TURNS EXECUTING THEIR MOVES UNTIL ONE PLAYER'S HP DROPS TO 0







TIPS AND TRICKS:


1. ELEMENTALS IS COMPLETELY BASED ON YOUR STRATEGY AND LUCK

• FIRE - Watch your health, the lower it is, the more you hurt so keep you opponents level down, save your mana for the finishing blow

• WATER - Your regeneration abilities are strong so shuffle in between offense and defense based on your opponent.

• EARTH - Your passive is your best friend. Just watch out for burst damage elements like fire

• WIND - You are always on defense, spam your basic attack until you have enough mana for your ultimate


2. USE CONSOLE TO CHECK PLAYERS' LIVES AFTER EVERY ROUND


3. PLAYER ONE ALWAYS GOES FIRST (USE THIS TO YOUR ADVANTAGE)

Our game concept was inspired by Pokemon. We took the turn-based combat and luck-based mechanics of Pokemon and incorporated them into our own game. Players engage in strategic battles where they take turns commanding their characters to use various skills and abilities.

Similar to Pokemon, each character in our game has unique abilities and attributes, and players must carefully choose their next move to outmaneuver their opponents. However, we've added our own twist to the game by introducing different characters, skills, moves, and passives.
