RULES:

ELEMENTALS IS A STRATEGICAL PLAYER VS PLAYER GAME


1. EACH PLAYER CHOOSES THEIR ELEMENTAL ATTRIBUTE WHICH HAVE THEIR OWN PASSIVE

• Fire: High DMG with No Heal Skill (Passive - Burning Soul: Deals extra DMG equal to 10%-20% of lost HP)

• Water: Decent Heal with Mid DMG (Passive - Sea Cradle: Heal effectiveness will increase as HP drops)

• Earth: Massive Heal with Low DMG (Passive - Magnitude: Deals 10-15 additional DMG every skill as HP drops)

• Wind: Well balanced all rounder (Passive - Windtalker: Heals 15% of current HP every successful move)


2. EACH ELEMENT HAVE 3 DIFFERENT SKILLS (1 BASIC AND 2 SPECIAL) WITH 2 DIFFERENT EFFECTS (HEAL AND DAMAGE)


3. EACH ATTACK WILL GO THROUGH A RANDOMIZER TO DETERMINE WHETHER IT WILL BE EFFECTIVE OR NOT (EACH MOVE HAS AN 80% CHANCE OF WORKING)


4. PLAYERS HAVE LIMITED MANA TO PREVENT SPAMMING (BASIC ATTACKS REPLENISH 10 MANA PER MOVE)


5. EACH PLAYER WILL TAKE TURNS EXECUTING THEIR MOVES UNTIL ONE PLAYER'S HP DROPS TO 0 OR BELOW 0




TIPS AND TRICKS:

1. ELEMENTALS IS COMPLETELY BASED ON YOUR STRATEGY AND LUCK

• EARTH counters FIRE

• FIRE counters WATER

• WATER counters WIND

• WIND counters EARTH


NOTE: THESE ARE JUST BASED ON ELEMENTAL STATS (THERE ARE OTHER VARIABLES THAT CAN AFFECT YOUR WINRATE)


2. USE CONSOLE TO CHECK PLAYERS' LIVES AFTER EVERY ROUND


3. PLAYER 1 ALWAYS GOES FIRST (USE THIS TO YOUR ADVANTAGE)
